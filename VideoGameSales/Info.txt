#Data Source: https://www.kaggle.com/gregorut/videogamesales
#Description: Dataset contains a list of video games with sales greater than 100,000 copies (through 2016)
#Fields: Rank, Name, Platform, Year, Genre, Publisher, NA_Sales, EU_Sales, JP_Sales, Other_Sales, Global_Sales
#Rank: Overall sales ranking
#Name: Name of the game
#Platform: Platform game was released on
#Year: Year the game was released
#Genre: Genre of the game
#Publisher: Publisher of the game
#NA_Sales: Sales in North America (in millions)
#EU_Sales: Sales in Europe (in millions)
#JP_Sales: Sales in Japan (in millions)
#Other_Sales: Sales in the rest of the world (in millions)
#Global_Sales: Total worldwide sales
